The road ahead

During the final discussion, we will trace roadmap for the proposal of a ADHO Special Interest Group (SIG) in Digital Literary Stylistics

Please find here the instructions for the proposal of a ADHO SIG

  • elect a contact person and then decide what focus to give to the SIG
  • set up work space and resources (such as web site, zotero db, tools)
  • decide on future meetings (at DH conferences, other, possible funding)
  • sketch basic structure of SIG
